# ISQA Knowledge Extraction Prompt

Use this prompt to extract ISQA conference material into QA governance principles without turning it into project-specific business truth.

## Required Output

- QA principles
- evidence rules
- defect classification rules
- testcase design principles
- risk-based testing principles
- release readiness principles
- stakeholder communication principles
- intern/task delegation principles
- what FALID may use directly
- what needs QA review
- what must not be treated as business rule

## Rules

- ISQA material is governance reference.
- ISQA must not define PGN business behavior.
- ISQA must not override MoM/BPMN/Figma/UI actual/stakeholder confirmation.
- ISQA may strengthen FALID discipline.
- Raw ISQA extraction is provisional until reviewed.
- Durable memory requires QA approval.
- If an ISQA point conflicts with controlled project evidence, route it to governance review rather than product truth.
